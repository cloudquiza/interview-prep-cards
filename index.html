How this works
  • Copy the prompt and paste it into ChatGPT. For the best experience (including generating a downloadable CSV file), make sure you’re logged into your ChatGPT account.
  • Customize the prompt: update the role name, adjust the number of questions, and optionally paste a full job posting for more targeted results.
  • Upload the generated interview_deck.csv (headers must be question,answer). You can also create your own CSV manually as long as it follows this schema.
  • Practice instantly: your cards appear as soon as the CSV is uploaded. Click the card to flip it, or use Prev / Next to move through the deck and start practicing right away.
  • Privacy: everything runs in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server.
  • Storage: your CSV isn’t saved permanently. Refreshing the page resets the deck.
